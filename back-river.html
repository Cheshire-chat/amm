Back River Gold

Sabina Gold & Silver

The Back River Gold project a series of gold deposits in banded iron formation, is located in SW Nunavut, Canada, approximately 520km northeast of Yellowknife, NWT. The project is currently focused on the George and Goose Property deposits which host significant gold resources. The project has a current preliminary economic assessment (May 2012) and is currently the subject of advanced engineering, baseline environmental studies and ongoing exploration.
