Diavik Diamond Mine - A21 Project

Located 300km NE of Yellowknife, the capital of Canada’s Northwest Territories, Diavik Diamond Mine began production in 2003 and became a fully underground mining operation in 2012. The mine produces predominantly gem-quality diamonds destined for high end jewellery. Just south of Diavik’s existing mining operations, A21 development requires rockfill dike construction to encircle the ore body offshore of existing mining operations at Lac de Gras.
