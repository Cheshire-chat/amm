Mount Milligan

AMEC

Mount Milligan is located approximately 145km NW of Prince George in central British Columbia, Canada. The Mount Milligan Mine is a conventional truck-shovel open-pit copper and gold mine and concentrator with a 62,500 tpd design capacity copper flotation processing plant. Mount Milligan has an estimated mine life of 22 years.
